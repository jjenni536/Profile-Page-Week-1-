Ladies and gentleman, the Prince of Afrobeats...

REMA

(Birth Name: Divine Ikubor)

The singer/song-writer was born May 1st, 2000 in Benin City, Nigeria (Edo Tribe). He rose to stardum after the release of his single "Dumebi". his sound is a collision of Middle-Eastern, Eastern Asian and west-African influnce.
